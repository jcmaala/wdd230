Where do we deliver?

One of the many places the Bountiful Foods deliver its fresh fruits and fruit drinks is Malibu. Malibu is famous for people who loves surfing and wanting to experience the tropical weather and the summber vibes this coastal city gives.

Nicknamed "The 'Bu" by surfers and locals, Malibu is famed for its strip of beaches (incorporated into the city proper in 1991), which includes Big Rock Beach, Broad Beach, County Line Beach, Dan Blocker Beach, La Costa Beach, Las Flores Beach, Malibu Beach, Point Dume Beach, Surfrider Beach, Topanga Beach, and Zuma Beach. State parks and beaches on the Malibu coast include Leo Carrillo State Beach and Park, Malibu Creek State Park, Point Mugu State Park, and Robert H. Meyer Memorial State Beach, with individual beaches such as El Matador Beach, El Pescador Beach, and La Piedra Beach. The many parks within the Santa Monica Mountains National Recreation Area lie along the ridges above the city along with local parks including Las Flores Creek Park, Legacy Park, Malibu Bluffs Park, and Trancas Canyon Park. source: (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malibu,_California)
