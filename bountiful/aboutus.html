About Us

Brief history

In 2013, Business owner Joe thought about delivering fruits to his neighborhood especially during summer since most of the people wants to feel refreshed. He also had an idea that what if instead of going to supermarkets and buy fruits there, people can just call someone to deliver it to their house directly, and they can also order a fruit drink of their choice without the hassle of traveling to the supermarkets. He then converted that idea into a business, which started as a small one, only covering his neighborhood. Until, as the business thrives, more and more people wanted to have fruits delivered to them until such time they delivered to the whole costal cities of southern california. Bountiful Foods continues to be committed on making sure that the fruits being delivered are farm-fresh and their service is top tier.
