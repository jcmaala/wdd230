Target Audience Description:

The target audience of this website are mainly people who wanted to have a financial assistance with their business, whether it's a big business or just small ones. This could be people from big companies who would like to promote their business in the local community or people who just want to save more money for their small business.

Personas:

Persona 1:

This person owns a huge business. His business is already popular in other places but he wants to expand it to more places. The chamber of commerce website will help him know that JC Chamber of Commerce specialize in such things.

Persona 2:

This person owns a small business and people in the local community knows his business. He wants to grow his business and he realize that in order to do so, he needs to have more money capital. The chamber of commerce website will also help him that the JC Chamber of Commerce gave opportunities for small business owners like him to borrow money to grow his business.

Scenarios:

This website can be useful to people when:

  • People who have business wants to boost their sales, and wants to contact a company that would enable them to do so.
  • People wants to improve their small business.
  • Someone wants to promote his/her business through the attractions of their community.
  • People wants to know about the policy of JC Chamber of Commerce for them to be able to apply as a member to experience its benefits.
  • People wants to learn more about Chamber of Commerce and how it would specifically benefit them.
